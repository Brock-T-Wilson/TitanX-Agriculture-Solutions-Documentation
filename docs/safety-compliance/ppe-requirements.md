# PPE Requirements

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) requirements help reduce injury risk during TitanX equipment operation, inspection, maintenance, and service activities.

All personnel must wear required PPE appropriate for the operational environment and assigned task.

## PPE Objectives

PPE requirements are intended to:

- Reduce exposure to operational hazards
- Improve workplace safety
- Protect personnel during equipment operation
- Support compliance with safety procedures
- Reduce injury severity during incidents

## Minimum Required PPE

The following PPE is required during standard equipment operation and inspection activities:

| PPE Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Safety Glasses | Required |
| Protective Gloves | Required |
| Steel-Toe Footwear | Required |
| High-Visibility Vest | Required during active field operations |
| Hearing Protection | Required during extended equipment operation |

Additional PPE may be required depending on operational or service conditions.

## Maintenance & Service PPE Requirements

Personnel performing maintenance or repair procedures may require additional PPE including:

- Face shields
- Chemical-resistant gloves
- Protective outerwear
- Respiratory protection
- Hydraulic fluid protection equipment

Review all applicable service procedures before beginning maintenance activities.

## Operational Safety Requirements

Personnel must:

- Inspect PPE before use
- Replace damaged PPE immediately
- Wear properly fitted PPE
- Follow all site-specific safety requirements
- Use task-specific protective equipment when required

Do not operate equipment without required protective equipment.

## High-Risk Operational Areas

Additional caution is required during:

- Hydraulic system service
- PTO inspection or repair
- Electrical troubleshooting
- Active field operations
- Heavy towing procedures
- Elevated noise environments

## PPE Inspection Requirements

Before beginning operational work:

1. Inspect safety glasses for damage.
2. Verify protective gloves are intact.
3. Confirm footwear is in acceptable condition.
4. Verify hearing protection functions correctly.
5. Replace damaged PPE immediately.

---

## Non-Compliance Guidance

Personnel who fail to follow PPE requirements may be removed from operational or service activities until safety compliance requirements are met.

## Escalation Guidance

Immediately escalate:

- Missing PPE
- Damaged protective equipment
- Unsafe operational conditions
- Repeated safety violations
- Hazard exposure concerns

Do not continue work during unresolved safety conditions.
