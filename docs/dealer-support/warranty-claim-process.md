# Warranty Claim Process

This procedure outlines the standard warranty claim process for TitanX equipment platforms.

Dealer service teams and authorized support personnel must follow these procedures when submitting warranty-related service requests, replacement part claims, or equipment defect reports.

## Process Objectives

The warranty claim process is intended to:

- Standardize warranty submissions
- Improve claim processing accuracy
- Reduce claim delays
- Document equipment defects consistently
- Support timely equipment service resolution

## Eligible Warranty Conditions

Warranty claims may apply to:

- Manufacturing defects
- Approved component failures
- Covered hydraulic system failures
- Electrical component defects
- PTO-related component failures
- Navigation system hardware failures

Warranty eligibility depends on equipment usage, service history, and warranty coverage terms.

## Required Documentation

Before submitting a warranty claim, dealers must collect:

| Required Information | Description |
|---|---|
| Equipment Model | TitanX equipment model number |
| Serial Number | Equipment serial identification |
| Equipment Hours | Current operational hours |
| Failure Description | Clear description of issue |
| Inspection Findings | Technician observations |
| Service History | Relevant maintenance records |
| Photos (if applicable) | Visible damage or failure evidence |

Incomplete submissions may delay claim approval.

## Warranty Claim Procedure

### 1. Verify Warranty Eligibility

Confirm:

- Equipment remains within warranty period
- Failure condition qualifies for coverage
- Required maintenance procedures were completed
- No unauthorized modifications exist

Do not submit claims without verifying eligibility requirements.

---

### 2. Perform Equipment Inspection

Qualified service personnel must inspect the equipment and document:

- Active fault conditions
- Visible damage
- Hydraulic or electrical concerns
- Operational failures
- Diagnostic findings

Document all observations clearly.

---

### 3. Record Failure Information

Document:

- Date of failure
- Equipment operating conditions
- Warning indicators
- Environmental conditions
- Troubleshooting steps completed

Accurate documentation improves claim review efficiency.

---

### 4. Submit Warranty Claim

Submit claims through approved TitanX dealer support channels.

Verify:

- All required fields are complete
- Documentation is attached properly
- Service records are included
- Supporting photos are uploaded when required

---

### 5. Await Claim Review

TitanX support personnel may:

- Request additional information
- Approve repairs
- Request additional inspection
- Deny claims outside coverage requirements

Do not replace major components without approval when authorization is required.

---

### 6. Complete Approved Repairs

After approval:

- Perform authorized repairs
- Use approved replacement components
- Follow TitanX service procedures
- Document completed repair actions

---

### 7. Finalize Claim Documentation

After repair completion:

- Record final service actions
- Document replacement components
- Update equipment service history
- Close claim documentation properly

## Escalation Guidance

Immediately escalate:

- Repeated component failures
- Structural equipment defects
- Safety-related failures
- Hydraulic rupture conditions
- Electrical fire hazards
- Warranty disputes requiring management review
