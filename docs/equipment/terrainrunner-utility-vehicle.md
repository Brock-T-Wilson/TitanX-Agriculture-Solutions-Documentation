# TerrainRunner Utility Vehicle

The TerrainRunner Utility Vehicle is a multi-purpose off-road support platform designed for agricultural transport, field service operations, equipment inspection, and terrain-based utility work.

The platform is commonly used by field supervisors, maintenance teams, and service technicians requiring reliable mobility across uneven terrain and active operational zones.

## Equipment Overview

The TerrainRunner platform supports:

- Field transportation
- Equipment inspection routes
- Utility material transport
- Service technician mobility
- Agricultural support operations
- Terrain navigation across off-road environments

The platform is engineered for durability, visibility, and operational efficiency during extended field use.

## Equipment Specifications

| Specification | Value |
|---|---|
| Engine Type | 4-Cylinder Diesel |
| Fuel Capacity | 14 gallons |
| Drive Type | 4WD |
| Passenger Capacity | 4 Operators |
| Maximum Payload | 1,500 lbs |
| Maximum Speed | 35 MPH |
| Ground Clearance | 13 inches |

## Primary Applications

The TerrainRunner Utility Vehicle is commonly used for:

- Field inspections
- Equipment transportation
- Tool and material movement
- Utility support operations
- Service technician travel
- Agricultural property navigation

## Standard Operating Requirements

Before operating the TerrainRunner platform:

1. Complete the required pre-operation inspection.
2. Verify tire condition and inflation levels.
3. Confirm fuel level and fluid status.
4. Inspect cargo security points.
5. Verify warning indicators are inactive before startup.
6. Wear all required PPE.

Do not exceed maximum payload limits or operate the vehicle in unsafe terrain conditions.

## Safety Information

Operators must follow all TitanX field safety procedures during TerrainRunner operation.

### Required PPE

- Safety glasses
- Protective footwear
- High-visibility vest during active field operations

:::warning
Do not operate the TerrainRunner Utility Vehicle on unstable terrain, steep inclines, or flooded operational areas.
:::
